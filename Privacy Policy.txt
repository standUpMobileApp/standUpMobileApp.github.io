Privacy Policy for the Stand Up mobile application (SUMA) 

Device Permissions
-The SUMA will require access to your device's camera for the purposes of augmented reality and screen capturing.
-The SUMA will require access to your device's storage for the purposes of saving your screen shots.

Data Usage
The SUMA stores screen shots to your device's storage at your request.
The SUMA does not store or transmit this information for any other purposes.
